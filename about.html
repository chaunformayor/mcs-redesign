Our History

25 Years of
Building Missouri

What started as a two-man roofing crew in 1999 has grown into one of Missouri's most respected full-service construction companies — and we're still family-run at heart.

1999

Founded

Mike and Dave started with a pickup truck and a roofing contract in St. Louis.

2004

Expanded to Remodeling

Added full interior remodeling services after demand from existing roofing clients.

2010

Licensed General Contractor

Obtained Missouri general contractor license, opening doors to commercial projects.

2016

Added Plumbing & Electrical

Brought licensed plumbers and electricians in-house for full-service delivery.

2024

500+ Projects Milestone

Celebrated completing our 500th project with a perfect safety record.
